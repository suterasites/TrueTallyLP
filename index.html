The Problem

The financial side of a health practice is genuinely complicated.

Between Medicare, private health funds, NDIS, mixed staff arrangements, and clinical software that doesn't talk directly to Xero, most bookkeepers aren't set up for it. We are.

Medicare & private health payments don't reconcile themselves

Bulk billing batch payments arrive days after the service date across multiple line items. Medibank, BUPA, HCF and NIB all pay differently. Getting this right matters for your BAS.

Payroll for a mix of practitioners and support staff

Employed practitioners, casual staff and contractors each carry different Modern Award obligations, superannuation, and STP requirements. One setup doesn't fit all.

Not all of your income is GST-free, and the line isn't obvious

Clinical services are exempt, but product sales, room hire, telehealth coaching and some NDIS services may not be. Misclassifying income causes BAS errors and ATO exposure.

Frequently asked

Frequently asked questions.

Do you work with Halaxy and Splose? +

Yes, we're familiar with both platforms and know how to pull payment data and reconcile it against your bank and Xero. If you're using a different practice management system, get in touch and we'll confirm whether we can work with it.

Are allied health services GST-free? +

Most clinical services provided by registered allied health practitioners are GST-free under the health services exemption. However non-clinical income, product sales, room hire, telehealth coaching, some NDIS services, may attract GST. Getting this right is critical for your BAS and something we handle carefully for every client.

How do you handle Medicare bulk billing reconciliation? +

Medicare pays in batches, often days after the service date, and the remittance doesn't always match what was billed line by line. We reconcile every Medicare payment against your Halaxy, Splose or practice software records so your income is correctly recorded and your BAS is accurate.

Can you handle payroll for a mix of employees and contractors? +

Yes. Allied health practices often have employed practitioners, casual support staff and independent contractors. We handle award interpretation under the Health Professionals and Support Services Award, STP reporting, superannuation and leave liability tracking.

Do you work with NDIS-funded services? +

Yes. We work with practices delivering NDIS-funded services, including those using Splose for plan management. We set up the right income categories in Xero and correctly classify plan-managed, agency-managed and self-managed clients.

Do you work remotely, or do I need to be based in Geelong? +

We work with practices across Victoria and New South Wales remotely. All bookkeeping is handled digitally via Xero, Halaxy or Splose integrations, and secure file sharing. Phone, video or email, whatever suits you.

How much does it cost? +

We charge a fixed monthly fee scoped to your practice, based on transaction volume, income streams, payroll headcount and BAS obligations. No hourly rates, no surprise invoices. We'll give you a clear quote after your free consultation.
